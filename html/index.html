Shaping the African Narrative Through the Lens

Extravaganza is a cinematic visual experience led by photographer and filmmaker Andrea Memba. Through storytelling, emotion, and intentional framing, this space celebrates Africa’s beauty, creativity, and power.

My Mission

To reshape and elevate Africa’s narrative using visual storytelling. Through photography and videography, I seek to document the continent’s identity, culture, struggles, victories, and hidden beauty.

Vision

A world where African stories are told authentically and powerfully by Africans. My vision is to inspire the next generation of storytellers, empower communities, and showcase Africa through a cinematic lens.
