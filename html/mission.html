Cinematic Storytelling for Africa

My mission is to reshape the African narrative through impactful photography and videography. I focus on authentic storytelling that highlights culture, identity, innovation, and humanity across the continent. This mission aligns with the Global Challenge of Art, Culture, and Design and the Global Opportunity of Creative Economy Development.

Alignment to GCGO

Through visual storytelling, I aim to:

  • Promote African creativity and cultural identity.
  • Showcase stories that challenge stereotypes.
  • Support grassroots artists and creative communities.
  • Enhance Africa’s visibility in global media.

Planned Impact

I plan to create a continent-wide visual archive of African stories—capturing everyday life, innovation, and beauty. My future goals include producing short films, hosting community photo workshops, and collaborating with African creatives. I aim to impact:

  • Young storytellers who want to learn visual arts.
  • Communities whose stories deserve global visibility.
  • The African creative industry through collaborative projects.
